Possesive Pronouns

I - MY
You - Your
He - His
She - Her
It - Its
We - Our
They - Their

Exercices
My "pc" is "a pc gamer"
Your "hair" is "beautifull"
His "teatcher" is "a good"
Her "mouth" is "large"
"the dog" is "in" its "class"
Our "gifts" are "ready"
Their "classes" are "so good"
